Introduction

My name is Valló Gábor. Although I've always been interested in programming (I wrote text adventure games during university back in the 1990s), I graduated from History and English language and started working as a teacher. I spent 20 years teaching in different secondary schools and also translating books. Over the years I felt a growing motivation to try my hand at something new. Finally in 2022 I got an opportunity: I went through the 4 weeks foundation course called Újratervezés 3.0. Here I could get an insight into the world of programming from the basics, and I instantly liked it. Therefore I went through Ruander's 10 months Junior Frontend developer course where I could consolidate my knowledge. I passed the vocational exam with an excellent result.

My hope is to find employment as a frontend programmer / website developer. This attracts me mainly because here I can use my creativity. Of course I'm also interested in backend, I also develop these skills. Fortunately nowadays a large amount of educational material is available on the net, so I can also learn by myself. I believe I can utilize the 'soft skills' I developed during my teaching career (e.g. communication, working alone or in a group, patience, precision) in this field as well.
